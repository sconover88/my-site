About
Crafting ideas
into experiences

I'm Scott, a designer with over eight years of experience helping brands find their voice and digital products discover their soul. My work spans brand identities, interface design, and visual systems built to last.

I partner with startups, agencies, and established companies who believe design isn't decoration — it's strategy made visible. Every project begins with listening and ends with something that feels inevitable.

When I'm not designing, I'm probably hunting through vinyl record shops, reading design history, or trying to convince people that negative space is always underrated.
